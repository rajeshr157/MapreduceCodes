Today almost all the Industries are getting digitally disrupted with the advent of new technologies and no company is immune to these changes. The age-old business models of reputed companies are technologically challenged by niche players and companies have to be agile to survive in this competitive environment. In order for the companies to be agile, the first and foremost requirement is to have people with right skill sets.

SanKir Technologies, an Institute in Digital Transformation Space is committed to bridge the skill gap in digital industry by providing high quality training with industry relevant Capstone project in Java, BIG DATA/Hadoop and Spark Framework. The programme is designed by professionals with 50+ years of industry experience and the sessions are conducted by experienced Architects and Subject Matter Experts.

Our Programme enables Fresh Graduates to be industry ready and it also enables experienced professionals to upskill themselves in Digital Transformation Space to accelerate their career growth.
